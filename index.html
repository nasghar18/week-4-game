Super Mario Guesser

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four items below. By clicking on an item you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win a game by matching your total score to the Target number. You lose a game if your total score goes above the Target number.

The value of each item is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the resets, the game will change the value of each item
